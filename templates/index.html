About NutriAgent AI

NutriAgent AI is an Agentic AI-based nutrition assistant designed to help users analyze their health profile, calculate BMI, estimate calorie requirements, and receive personalized nutrition guidance based on their goals. The project combines modern web development and AI-driven decision making to provide a smart nutrition planning experience.
